# Data Analysis: Performance Evaluation of an E-commerce Business "Showz" (Event Ticket Sales Company)

This project offers an exhaustive analysis of the performance of an e-commerce platform. The objective is to evaluate key business metrics related to user activity, sales, and marketing efficiency to identify strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.

## Objective
Evaluate the overall health of the e-commerce platform by analyzing:
- User engagement and retention.
- Sales performance and customer lifetime value.
- The efficiency and profitability of marketing investments.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- **Python:** Pandas, NumPy (for data manipulation)
- **Jupyter Notebook:** Interactive environment for analysis.
- **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn
- **CSV Datasets:** Contain records of user visits, orders, and marketing costs.

## Key Steps
1.  **Data Preparation:**
    - Three datasets were loaded and cleaned: `visits_log_us.csv`, `orders_log_us.csv`, and `costs_us.csv`.
    - Data types were converted and new features were created (e.g., `session_duration`, `cohort_month`) for deeper analysis.
2.  **User Activity and Retention Analysis:**
    - Daily Active Users (DAU), Weekly Active Users (WAU), and Monthly Active Users (MAU) were calculated to understand the user scale.
    - A cohort analysis was performed to track user retention over time.
3.  **Sales and Customer Value Analysis:**
    - Purchase behavior was analyzed, including time to first purchase and average order value.
    - Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) was calculated to understand long-term revenue generated by user cohort.
4.  **Marketing Efficiency Analysis:**
    - Total marketing spend and Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by source were calculated.
    - Return on Marketing Investment (ROMI) was analyzed on a monthly basis.

## Results
The analysis confirms that while the platform has a strong and active user base, there are significant challenges in user retention and marketing profitability:

*   **User Activity:** The platform has a strong user base with a **DAU of ~907**, a **WAU of ~5,800**, and an **MAU of ~23,000**. Users perform an average of one session per day with a duration of **11 minutes**.
*   **Retention:** User retention is a significant challenge. Retention drops to less than **10%** in the first month and remains consistently low in the following months.
*   **Monetization:** The average purchase size is **$5.00**. The average time to a user's first purchase is **17 days**. The **LTV** of the most recent user cohorts is lower than previous ones, indicating a decline in user value over time.
*   **Marketing Efficiency:** Total marketing spend was **$329k**. The **CAC varies significantly** by source, with sources 9 ($0.59), 4 ($0.73), and 10 ($0.72) being the most efficient. Critically, the **monthly ROMI was consistently negative**, meaning marketing spend did not generate enough revenue to be profitable.
